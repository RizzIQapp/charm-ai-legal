Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Charm AI collects the following information to provide our services:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your name and email address.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, practice sessions, and session counts.
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, and app version.
  • AI Conversation Data: Text entered during practice scenarios is processed in real-time and not stored permanently.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Providing and personalizing the Charm AI experience
  • Tracking practice streaks and session history
  • Processing subscription payments via RevenueCat
  • Sending optional push notifications (only with your permission)
  • Improving app features and fixing bugs
  • Responding to support requests

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with trusted providers:

  • OpenAI: AI conversation processing
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and payment management
  • Apple: Sign in with Apple authentication

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data while your account is active. AI session content is not stored beyond the active session. Deleting your account removes your personal data within 30 days.

5. Children's Privacy

Charm AI is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 17 and will delete such data if discovered.

6. Your Rights

You may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption. No internet transmission is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy and will notify you of significant changes via the app or this page.
