FAQ
Frequently asked questions

Is Charm AI free?

Yes, the core app is free to download and use. You get 2 practice sessions per day on the free tier. Charm AI Pro unlocks unlimited sessions, all 6 characters, and priority features.

Who is Charm AI for?

Charm AI is built for women who want to feel more confident and magnetic in real conversations and dating situations. It's a practice tool, not a dating app.

Are the AI characters real people?

No. All AI characters in Charm AI are entirely fictional. They are designed for entertainment and practice purposes only and do not represent real individuals.

How much does Charm AI Pro cost?

Charm AI Pro is available for $49.99/year. You can cancel anytime through your Apple ID subscription settings.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We do not sell your personal information. Conversation data is processed in real-time and not stored permanently. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
